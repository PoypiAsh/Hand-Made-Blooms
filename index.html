About Us

Our Story

Handmade Blooms was established in 2025 by an independent woman with a deep passion for floral art and creativity. What started as a small hobby of making handcrafted flowers soon grew into a meaningful business that brings joy to others.


We create eco-friendly, handcrafted flower arrangements made from fuzzy wires. Each design is carefully made by hand to give a personal and creative touch. Perfect for gifts, home decorations, and special events.
